Our Story

Setia Trafik was established in 2015 with a clear mission: to raise the standard of traffic management in Malaysia.

Founded 2015

Started as a small team of certified traffic managers in Kuala Lumpur, handling utility and drainage works around the Klang Valley. Our founding principle was simple — hire only certified people, own our equipment, and never cut corners on safety.

Growth Milestones

  • 2015 — Company founded, first JKR contract awarded
  • 2017 — CIDB Grade G5 registered, first highway expressway project
  • 2019 — MOF registration obtained, fleet expanded to 15 vehicles
  • 2021 — East Malaysia operations launched (Sabah & Sarawak)
  • 2024 — PKK/STB & SPKK certifications, Bumiputera status recognised
  • 2026 — 500+ projects, 50+ personnel, nationwide coverage

Our Vision

To be Malaysia's most trusted and comprehensive traffic management company — where every project is safer because we were there.
